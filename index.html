Welcome to Zero Qualms

This is a project for persons that

  1. enjoy thinking about problems, but work to not cause them;
  2. see that people may gain great rewards as a result of solving problems, or see that "problems" are also opportunities; and
  3. proactively think of and seek to mitigate future problems and profit from the opportunities that they present.

If you enjoy thinking about, analyzing, and mitigating problems and capitalizing upon opportunities, join us.

For more thinking along these lines, please refer to @tenkabuto's blog: http://brandon.zeroqualms.net.

If you wish to contact us, please contact @tenkabuto via the contact options here: http://brandon.zeroqualms.net/about/#contact-me

Thank you!
